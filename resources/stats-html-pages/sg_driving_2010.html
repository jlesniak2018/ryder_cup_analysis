The number of strokes a player takes from a specific distance off the tee on Par 4 and par 5's is measured against a statistical baseline to determine the player's strokes gained or lost off the tee on a hole. The sum of the values for all holes played in a round minus the field average strokes gained/lost for the round is the player's Strokes gained/lost for that round. The sum of strokes gained for each round are divided by total rounds played. (2567)
